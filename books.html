My favourite books :



U4

U4 is a series of post-apocalyptic, French novels published by Nathan/ Syros. The peculiarity of the series is that the first four volumes, although written by four different authors, form a single story and can be read in the order of its choice. Indeed each book describes the point of view of a single character...

Synopsys:
A filovirus, called U4 (U for Utrecht which is the city of the Netherlands in which it appeared, and 4 for "4th generation") and appeared in mid-October, decimated in a few days nearly 90% of the population. The survivors are mainly adolescents between 15 and 18 years of age (due to a defective vaccine that several age groups had received a few years earlier), some military (protected by infrastructure) and very rare individuals of various ages (for more mysterious reasons).

For the four, a crossing of France plunged into chaos began, where delinquent gangs, authorities ready for the worst to restore order and survivors decided to defend their independence at all costs. All will eventually meet but their destiny will be different.
