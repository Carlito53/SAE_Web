My favourite movies :



The Road

The Road is a post-apocalyptic drama film. Directed by John Hillcoat, released in 2009. It is based on the novel of the same name written by Cormac McCarthy.

Synopsys:
In a post-apocalyptic land ravaged by a cataclysm of unknown origin, animals and plants disappear while a few humans try to survive.
In this landscape of ashes where cold and hunger reign, the greatest danger is cannibalism. A man and his young son want to join the sea heading south.
But on these desolate roads where barbarism has resumed its rights, we must find hope of survival.



Southpaw

Southpaw is a drama film directed by Antoine Fuqua and released in 2015.

Synopsys:
World boxing champion Billy Hope leads a lavish existence with his beautiful wife and daughter whom he loves more than anything.
When his wife is killed, his world crumbles, losing his home and his fortune.
Worse, the custody of his daughter is withdrawn from him, the justice considering his behavior incompatible with his role of father.
Down below, he finds invaluable help in the person of Tick Willis, a former boxer with whom he resumes training.
Billy will have to fight to find the way to redemption and thus regain custody of his daughter.
